About this space

Escape to the serene beauty of the Siwa Oasis at our Star Camp. Nestled between the Great Sand Sea and lush palm groves, our campsite offers a perfect blend of traditional Bedouin hospitality and modern glamping comfort. Whether you want to sleep under the stars or in our luxury huts, the magic of the desert awaits.

Enjoy evenings by the bonfire, guided treks to the salt lakes, and organic meals prepared with local ingredients. Perfect for solo travelers, couples, and small groups looking to disconnect.
